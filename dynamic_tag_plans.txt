to set a tag:
	type /tag <tag>
	list of tags with descriptions appears
	you select a tag, it applies to you
to delete a tag:
	/untag|remove_tag <tag>
	list of your tags with descriptions appears
	you select a tag, it deletes from you
to clear all tags:
	/clear
to add new tag:
	/add_tag <tag> <description>
to see all tags:
	/all_tags
to see my tags:
	/my_tags
